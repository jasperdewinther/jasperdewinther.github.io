Advanced Graphics @UU

This course was one of the major reasons why I wanted to do my master’s at Utrecht University, and it delivered. For the first time, I could work on a ray tracer with the help of a very good and dedicated lecturer, namely Jacco Bikker. During the course, we first implemented a Whitted style ray tracer (on the CPU). I was pleasantly surprised with the performance, as I thought nothing could be rendered (let alone ray traced) on the CPU.
